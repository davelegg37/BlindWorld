Welcome to Wheelchair World may get a glimpse into the life of the disabled through wheelchair. In this world you must accomplish an assortment of household tasks from your power wheelchair which you control with your joystick. In the game you have eight tasks to perform. For each task you accomplish you receive two task points. To accomplish a task you must maneuver your wheelchair through the house to the appropriate object and engage it in the correct Â alignment. When you have accomplished all your tasks the game ends and your score will be your total task points minus your frustration points. Life is full of frustrations and if you live in wheelchair world you have more than your share. Each time you unintentionally hit an object or wall not related to your task you receive a frustration point and have to start all over. If you fail to accomplish your goal within the time limit you get a frustration point and have to start over.
